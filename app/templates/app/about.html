About Pyum

Pyum (Pie-Yum) is all about finding you a healthy meal to eat. We're a nutritional recipe aggregator. After filling some information about your health and activity level, we attempt to find you something to eat that will help you achieve your goal weight. Our recipe search is powered by Yummly. After specifying what ingredients you would or would not like to have, 25 recipes are returned back, each linking to nutritional information and steps to prepare that meal.


Calorie Calculations

The two calculations we use are the following:

Female = 655 + (4.35 * weight) + (4.7 * height) - (4.7 * age)

Male = 66 + (6.23 * weight) + (12.7 * height) - (6.8 * age)

We then use your activity level and your weekly goal to calculate how many calories you require each day. When you search for a meal and say how many calories you have already consumed, we take that number and subtract it from your determined total. We then divide by how many meals that you specify you have left in the day, and we use that final number to search for recipes.


Type II Diabetic

If you specified that you are a type II diabetic, we add in a few nutritional requirements for any meal given to you. Carbs are now limited in a range between 45 - 65 grams, and sodium is now limited to a maximum of 0.4 grams.
